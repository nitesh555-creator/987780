Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a boating license to hire a boat?

No, Aqua Marina offers self-drive boats that do not require a boating licence. However, you must be at least 18 years old to hire a boat


Can I fish from the boat?

Yes, fishing is allowed from our boats.

What safety equipment is provided on the boat?

All boats come with life jackets, a first aid kit, and safety instructions. Our staff will give you a safety briefing before departure.


Can we bring alcohol on the boat?

Yes, you can bring alcohol, however, the boat driver must not drink while operating the boat. Safety is our top priority.


What should I do in an emergency?

In case of an emergency, use the flares and marine radio, to call for help. Additionally, please contact our marina staff immediately for assistance.
