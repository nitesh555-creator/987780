About Us

At Aqua Marina, we offer a fun and easy way for you to explore the water with our self-drive boats—no license or experience needed! Whether you're looking for a peaceful day of fishing, a family outing, or a romantic adventure, our boats are perfect for any occasion. Why Choose Us? No License Required: Anyone over 18 can captain our easy-to-use boats. Safety First: We provide all the necessary safety gear and a quick briefing before you set off. Affordable & Flexible: Enjoy competitive rates with no hidden fees, and explore at your own pace. Variety of Boats: We offer boats for couples, families, and groups, so there’s something for everyone.
